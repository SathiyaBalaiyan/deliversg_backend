Australia Rider Privacy Policy

 

Roofoods Limited and Deliveroo Australia Pty Ltd ("we", "our" or "Deliveroo") are committed to protecting the privacy of rider applicants, riders and delegates, including all users of the rider mobile device application (the "Rider App").

This privacy policy applies to rider applicants, riders and delegates (to the extent that we are able to identify you, except for sections 1.1, 1.2, 2.1 and 2.2, which do not apply to delegates) (“you” or “your”). If you are a rider, you must ensure that your delegate has read this privacy policy before riding with us.

Please read the following privacy policy which explains how we use and protect your information.

1. INFORMATION THAT WE COLLECT FROM YOU

 

Your rider application

 

    When you apply to ride with Deliveroo, we ask you to provide information about yourself including your name, contact details, vehicle type, date of birth, postcode, your right to work in Australia as an independent contractor, any unspent convictions and whether you’ve worked for us before.

    During the application process, we (or a third party) will ask you to provide further information and documents, such as a copy of your passport, driver licence, visa, insurance and proof of address for background checks and right to work checks. We will also track the progress of your application and record the results of your background and right to work checks.

Your account and ride planning

    For your account set-up and management, we may ask you to provide financial, tax and business registration information, including your ABN. We record your rider payment arrangement, a copy of your signed Supplier Agreement and we create a Rider ID for you. We may also obtain or create further information related to your services for Deliveroo, such as your vehicle registration, address, contact details of your next of kin (whose details you have permission to share with us for emergency purposes), where you want to work, your kit, fees, invoices, tips if obtained via the Rider App and photo.

    Through the Rider App or through a third party provider, you tell us when you want to ride for us, and we keep a log of your deliveries.

 

Your location

 

    When you request to use a location-based function in the Rider App while your status is set to “offline” (i.e. not available for orders), for example, when you want to find a zone centre or use the zone selector control, we may access your location from your device in order to provide this functionality. If you do not want us to view your location when you are “offline”, please do not use these functions.

    When your status is set to “online” (i.e. available for orders) on the Rider App, we intermittently collect your location data and information about your rider activities from your device, including any actions you take which may increase or decrease the efficiency of the network (also see section 2.5).

Your use of the Rider App and Rider Support

    When you use the Rider App at any time, we collect information about your usage of the Rider App and about your rider and order status. We may also collect technical information from your device that may affect the technical functionality or performance of the Rider App on your device, for example, certain characteristics of and performance data about your device, Rider App and carrier/operating system, including your device type, connection type, app version, phone language, screen size and platform. Unless you have elected to remain anonymous through your device and/or platform settings, this information may be collected and used by us automatically and we may use cookies or similar technologies when we collect and process this data.

    When you interact with our rider support function (“Rider Support”) our team may collect and use information about you and your services for Deliveroo, and any queries you may have. If you contact Rider Support by instant chat (if available), your messages will be recorded. If you contact Rider Support by phone, your calls will be recorded for internal Rider Support training purposes.

 

Information about your services

    We may receive information about your services from the restaurants who work with us (our "Partner Restaurants") or our customers. Our Rider Support or Customer Services teams may also process incident reports from you, our Partner Restaurants, our customers or other members of the public containing the circumstances of any incident you may have been involved with.

    We may collect information about you from the messages, feedback or letters you send to us or provide to us through focus groups or surveys.

 

Providing your information in other ways

 

By accessing the Rider App using other digital routes such as (but not limited to) a tablet or other devices/technology, then Deliveroo's data collection and usage as set out in this privacy policy will apply in that context too.

    When you use websites or applications of third parties in connection with your services for Deliveroo, including during the application and on-boarding process, those sites or applications may collect and process your information independently of Deliveroo. We recommend that you check their privacy policies to understand how they process your personal data.

 

If you do not provide your information

 

    If you do not provide some or all of the requested information, Deliveroo may be unable to accept your application or to complete set-up or manage your account.

 

2. USE OF YOUR INFORMATION

 

Processing your application and managing your account and payments

 

    We use your personal information to review your suitability to be a rider with Deliveroo, including to undertake background and right to work checks, and if you are successful, to on-board you as a rider with us. We also use this personal information to create and administer your Deliveroo rider account and to contact you in connection with your application, Deliveroo’s rider operations, your rider services, incentives and for other purposes in connection with your services to Deliveroo.

    We use your personal information, such as your finance and business information and records of your deliveries to calculate and administer your payment and pay you for rider services that you provide to Deliveroo.

 

Operating and improving our business

 

    We use your account information and the times you want to ride for logistics planning purposes.

    We use some of your personal information, including your vehicle type, location data, Rider App usage and phone number, to provide live Deliveroo operations to our Partner Restaurants and customers.

    Information we collect about your attendance, your super peak participation and whether you’ve cancelled sessions with less than 24 hours notice will be used to determine your level of priority access to booking. If your rider actions indicate that you may be fraudulently interacting with our system or otherwise seriously or materially breaching your Supplier Agreement, we may manually or automatically disable your access pending a review. We may use your data to decide to terminate your services with Deliveroo if we have the right to terminate under your Supplier Agreement. Please speak to our Rider Support agents if you would like to discuss or contest any of these decisions.

    We may use information we collect from you, including your usage of the Rider App, communications and incentives we send to you and, as well as your location, rider activities and feedback you provide, to develop and improve our business, including our app, website, platform, operational efficiency, customer experience and rider experience and safety, including for any statistical and analytical purposes and to prevent fraud or other misuse of our platform.

    We use technical information relating to your device, including any information obtained using cookies, to fix any bugs or other technical issues in the Rider App, for API authentication, to enable auto-login and to enable the Rider App to function on iOS and Android.

 

Providing support and upholding your obligations

    Our Rider Support teams may need to use your information to resolve any issues you are having in connection with the services you are providing to Deliveroo, including any payment queries you may have.

    We may use your information, including incident reports, to investigate any complaints or potential non-compliance with your Supplier Agreement.

 

Media campaigns
